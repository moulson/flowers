Flowers from the Garden is based in Harrogate and Elizabeth aims to grow as many seasonal blooms as time and nature permit. Sweet peas, dahlias, peonies, roses and tulips abound in their seasons interspersed with all manner of annual, biennial and perennial flowers and foliage.

Once grown the flowers are sold to florists, local farm shops and other customers who appreciate the seasonality of flowers and the value of them being grown locally.
