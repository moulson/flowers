Flowers for Florists

By building relationships over time we develop a real understanding of individual style and floral requirements. We are happy to arrange visits to our growing areas and we endeavour to reflect your requirements in our planting schedules.

Event Flowers

If you are organising a family birthday party, christening or any other special occasion celebration where you would like flowers to feature, we can provide you with the flowers to arrange yourself. We can also prepare the table decorations for you using our fresh flowers from the garden.

Gift Bouquets

Locally grown seasonal flowers are a lovely gift for all sorts of occasions. We can make up gift bouquets using the flowers we have grown for delivery in the Harrogate area.

Cut Flowers

Nothing makes us happier than seeing the flowers we've grown adorn the kitchen table in the heart of a home. No requirement is too small so even if you would simply like a bunch of tulips or sweet peas, let us know and we'll have them ready for you to collect.

Bereavement

We offer a special service for those who have recently lost a loved one who perhaps had a passion for flowers and gardening and would have appreciated a tribute prepared using seasonal flowers.
