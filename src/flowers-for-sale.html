Flowers for Florists

By building relationships with florists over time we develop a real understanding of their individual style and floral requirements. We grow to order where possible and offer wholesale prices with no minimum order requirement. We are happy to arrange visits to our growing areas.

Wedding and Event Flowers

We work with talented floral designers and can also supply buckets of locally grown seasonal flowers and foliage in your colours for you to arrange yourself. We can discuss flower varieties and make recommendations for weddings and other special events such as family birthday parties and christenings.

Gift Bouquets

Locally grown seasonal flowers are a lovely gift for all sorts of occasions. We can make up gift bouquets using the flowers we have grown for delivery in the Harrogate area.

Bereavement

We offer a special service for those who have recently lost a loved one who had a passion for flowers and gardening and would like to have a tribute prepared using seasonal flowers.

Cut Flowers

Nothing makes us happier than seeing the flowers we've grown adorn the kitchen table in the heart of a home. No requirement is too small so even if you'd simply like a bunch of tulips or sweet peas, let us know and we'll have them ready for you to collect.

Garden Lovers

When we're not tending to our flowers we'll be out tending to our garden. We are blessed with a beautiful garden set in an interesting topographic and an array of special features. We welcome visits from photography and gardening groups by arrangement.
